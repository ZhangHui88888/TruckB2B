## Key Considerations When Sourcing

### 1. Supplier Verification

Before placing orders, verify:

- **Factory existence** - Request photos/videos or visit in person
- **Certifications** - ISO, IATF 16949, product certifications
- **Export experience** - Years in business, major markets served
- **References** - Contact existing customers

### 2. Quality Assurance

Ensure quality through:

- **Sample testing** - Always test samples before bulk orders
- **Third-party inspection** - Use services like SGS, Bureau Veritas
- **Clear specifications** - Provide detailed requirements
- **Quality agreements** - Define acceptable defect rates

### 3. Communication

Effective communication is crucial:

- Use clear, simple English
- Provide detailed drawings and specifications
- Confirm all details in writing
- Establish regular update schedules

## Understanding Pricing

### Price Components

### Negotiation Tips

1. **Order volume** - Larger orders get better prices
2. **Payment terms** - Flexible terms may cost more
3. **Shipping method** - Sea freight vs air freight
4. **Long-term relationship** - Loyal customers get priority

## Shipping and Logistics

### Common Shipping Methods

| Method | Transit Time | Best For |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| Sea freight | 25-40 days | Large orders, cost-sensitive |
| Air freight | 5-10 days | Urgent orders, small volume |
| Rail freight | 15-20 days | Europe, balanced option |
| Express courier | 3-7 days | Samples, very urgent |

### Incoterms to Know

- **FOB** - Free On Board (you arrange shipping)
- **CIF** - Cost, Insurance, Freight included
- **DDP** - Delivered Duty Paid (door-to-door)

## Common Challenges and Solutions

### Challenge 1: Quality Inconsistency

**Solution:** Implement incoming quality inspection and work with certified suppliers.

### Challenge 2: Communication Barriers

**Solution:** Use clear documentation, video calls, and consider hiring a sourcing agent.

### Challenge 3: Long Lead Times

**Solution:** Plan ahead, maintain safety stock, and build relationships for priority treatment.

### Challenge 4: Intellectual Property

**Solution:** Work with reputable suppliers, use NDAs, and register trademarks in China.
