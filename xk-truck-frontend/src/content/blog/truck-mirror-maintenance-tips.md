## Why Mirror Maintenance Matters

Truck mirrors are critical safety components. Properly maintained mirrors help drivers:

- Monitor blind spots effectively
- Make safe lane changes
- Reverse and maneuver safely
- Comply with transportation regulations

## Common Mirror Problems

### 1. Vibration and Looseness

**Causes:**
- Worn mounting brackets
- Loose bolts and fasteners
- Damaged vibration dampeners

**Solutions:**
- Regular tightening of all fasteners
- Replace worn mounting hardware
- Install quality dampening components

### 2. Fogging and Moisture

**Causes:**
- Damaged seals
- Cracked housing
- Temperature fluctuations

**Solutions:**
- Check and replace seals regularly
- Use heated mirrors in cold climates
- Apply anti-fog treatments

### 3. Scratches and Damage

**Prevention:**
- Train drivers on proper adjustment
- Use protective covers when parked
- Address minor damage promptly

## When to Replace Mirrors

Replace your truck mirrors when you notice:

- Cracks in the glass or housing
- Persistent vibration despite repairs
- Heating element failure (for heated mirrors)
- Adjustment mechanism malfunction
- Significant discoloration or hazing

## Choosing Replacement Mirrors

### Quality Indicators

Look for mirrors with:
- **OEM-equivalent specifications**
- **Proper certification marks**
- **Quality glass with accurate reflection**
- **Durable housing materials**
- **Reliable heating elements** (if applicable)
